Modern Connections

The Industrial Revolution’s inventions never disappeared—they evolved. The same search for efficiency that powered steam engines now drives renewable energy. The electrification that once lit up cities now fuels our digital world. The medical breakthroughs that started in small laboratories now guide genetic engineering and global health systems.

Each step of progress builds on what came before. Understanding that link between past and present helps us see that innovation isn’t just about technology—it’s about how we use it to shape human life.
